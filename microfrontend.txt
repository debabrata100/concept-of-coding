# Advantages of micro front end
- Speed up the development process
- Faster production rollout
- Easier code reusability
- Reduces Dependancy
- Possibility of using dfferent technology
- allow multiple teams to work independently, choose their technology stacks, and establish release cycles

# Drawback
- Lack of control
- More coordination required(contracts)
- Complex development & development lfecycle
