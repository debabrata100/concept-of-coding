# What Is HashiCorp Vault?
HashiCorp Vault is a secrets management and data protection tool designed to secure, store, and control access to sensitive information—often referred to as secrets—across various environments, including on-premises, cloud, and hybrid infrastructures.

## Core Capabilities & Features

* Centralized Secrets Management
Vault provides a unified interface for managing secrets like API keys, credentials, certificates, and encryption keys, with strong access control and auditing built-in.

HashiCorp Developer

* Dynamic Secrets
It can generate "just-in-time" credentials (e.g., for AWS, databases) that are time-limited and automatically revoked—reducing the risk of credential misuse.

* Encryption as a Service
Vault can encrypt and decrypt data on demand without storing the raw data, enabling teams to offload encryption complexity securely.

* Identity-Based Access Control
Access is governed by authenticated identities—be they human or machine—leveraging auth methods like tokens, LDAP, AppRole, GitHub, and more, with granular policy enforcement.
